## About the Studio

American Skin Art is North Tonawanda's custom tattoo studio, specializing in professional, high-quality tattoo work. Every tattoo starts with a conversation, ensuring custom designs that are done with safety, care, and longevity in mind.

### History
From apprentice to owner, Devin Goff has dedicated his career to North Tonawanda's oldest tattoo shop. After working alongside the original owner for years, Devin stepped up to ensure the business didn't just survive, but thrived. He is currently overseeing the construction of a new custom facility located directly behind the current shop.

### Mission
This expansion represents the studio's commitment to the community: keeping the history alive while upgrading the experience with a fresh, sterile, and professional environment.
