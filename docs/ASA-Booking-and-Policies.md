# American Skin Art - Booking & Policies

## Booking Process

### Consultation Request
Consultations help plan size, placement, and budget for your tattoo.

**How to Book:**
1. Visit website: americanskinart.com
2. Navigate to "Book a Consult" section
3. Fill out the booking form with:
   - Your name
   - Email address
   - Phone number (optional)
   - Tattoo placement (e.g., forearm)
   - Preferred artist (Devin Goff, Danny Peltier, Dale Murray, or No Preference)
   - Detailed description of your idea
   - Size in inches
   - Black & grey or color preference
   - Reference links

**Alternative Contact Methods:**
- **Phone:** (716) 694-9185
- **Email:** admin@americanskinart.com
- **In Person:** 44 Webster St, North Tonawanda, NY 14120

**Response Time:**
You'll receive a reply with next steps. Please include references and preferred days/times in your request.

## Deposit Policy

### Non-Refundable Deposit
- **Required:** A non-refundable deposit is required to hold your appointment
- **Application:** Deposit applies to the final session cost
- **Purpose:** Secures your appointment time and artist availability

## Cancellation & Rescheduling

### Advance Notice Required
- **48-Hour Notice:** Required to reschedule your appointment
- **No-Shows:** Forfeit the deposit
- **Rescheduling:** Contact the studio as soon as possible if you need to change your appointment

## Age Requirements

### Minimum Age
- **18+ Required:** You must be 18 years or older for tattoos
- **Valid ID:** Bring a valid government-issued photo ID
- **No Minors:** No minors are allowed in the procedure area

## Appointment Preparation

### What to Bring
1. Valid photo ID (driver's license, passport, state ID)
2. Reference images (if applicable)
3. Completed consent form (available at americanskinart.paperform.co)

### How to Prepare
- **Get Rest:** Arrive well-rested
- **Hydrate:** Drink plenty of water before your appointment
- **Eat:** Have a meal before arriving
- **Avoid:** Alcohol and blood-thinning medications (unless prescribed)
- **Clothing:** Wear comfortable clothing that allows easy access to the tattoo area

## Aftercare Policy

### Initial Care (First 3-5 Days)
1. **Wash Gently:**
   - Use antibacterial soap
   - Lukewarm water
   - Pat dry with clean towel

2. **Apply Ointment:**
   - Thin layer of recommended ointment
   - 2-3 times daily
   - Don't over-apply

3. **Avoid:**
   - Direct sunlight
   - Swimming pools
   - Hot tubs
   - Soaking in bathtubs
   - Picking or scratching

### Long-Term Care
- Follow studio-provided aftercare instructions
- Keep tattoo moisturized
- Use sunscreen when healed
- Contact studio with any concerns
