# American Skin Art - Artist Profiles

## Devin Goff - Owner & Lead Artist

### Contact & Portfolio
- **Instagram:** @sinisterblack_tattoos
- **Instagram URL:** https://instagram.com/sinisterblack_tattoos
- **Profile Page:** americanskinart.com/artists/sinisterblack_tattoos.html

### Biography
From apprentice to owner, Devin Goff has dedicated his career to North Tonawanda's oldest tattoo shop. After working alongside the original owner for years, Devin stepped up to ensure the business didn't just survive, but thrived. He is currently overseeing the construction of a new custom facility located directly behind the current shop. This expansion represents his commitment to the community: keeping the history alive while upgrading the experience with a fresh, sterile, and professional environment.

### Specialties & Style
**Primary Styles:**
- Bold black & grey tattoos
- Heavy contrast work
- Graphic imagery with classic tattoo feel
- Custom pieces designed for longevity

**Approach:**
Devin focuses on custom pieces that age well and read clearly from across the room. His work emphasizes strong, bold imagery with clean execution and professional quality.

**Best For:**
- Large scale projects
- Bold graphic designs
- Black & grey realism
- Custom traditional-influenced work
- Clients seeking impactful, long-lasting tattoos

### Booking with Devin
- Available Tuesday - Saturday, 12pm - 8pm
- Book via website: americanskinart.com with "Devin Goff" selected as preferred artist
- Consultation recommended for all custom work

## Dale Murray - Tattoo Artist

### Contact & Portfolio
- **Instagram:** @cruelbloomtattoo
- **Instagram URL:** https://instagram.com/cruelbloomtattoo
- **Profile Page:** americanskinart.com/artists/cruelbloomtattoo.html

### Biography
Dale Murray specializes in creating organic, flowing tattoo designs with exceptional attention to detail. His work is characterized by fine detail, floral elements, and softer black & grey pieces that emphasize texture, movement, and natural flow on the body.

### Specialties & Style
**Primary Styles:**
- Fine detail work
- Floral elements
- Softer black & grey pieces
- Organic and botanical designs
- Textured, flowing compositions

**Approach:**
Dale's work leans into texture, movement, and designs that sit naturally on the body's contours. He excels at creating pieces that feel like they belong on the skin, with strong attention to how the design flows with anatomy.

**Best For:**
- Floral and botanical tattoos
- Organic, flowing designs
- Fine detail work
- Softer black & grey aesthetics
- Textured pieces
- Clients seeking natural, graceful designs

## Choosing Your Artist

### By Style Preference

**Bold & Graphic:**
→ Book with **Devin Goff**
- Heavy contrast black & grey
- Large scale projects
- Classic tattoo feel

**Illustrative & Pop Culture:**
→ Book with **Danny Peltier**
- Neo-traditional
- Clean linework
- Fan art and references

**Floral & Organic:**
→ Book with **Dale Murray**
- Botanical designs
- Fine detail
- Flowing, natural compositions

### No Preference?
Select "No Preference" when booking and the studio will match you with the best artist for your project based on:
- Design style
- Size and placement
- Artist availability
- Complexity and detail level

### Consultation Process
All artists offer consultations to:
- Discuss your vision
- Review reference materials
- Determine size and placement
- Provide pricing estimate
- Plan session timeline
- Answer questions
