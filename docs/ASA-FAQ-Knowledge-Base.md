## Booking & Appointments

### How do I book an appointment?
1. Visit americanskinart.com
2. Click "Book a Consult"
3. Fill out the consultation form with your idea, placement, size, and preferred artist
4. Include reference images and preferred dates/times
5. Submit and wait for a response with next steps

### Do I need a consultation first?
Consultations are recommended for custom work. They help us plan:
- Design details
- Size and placement
- Pricing estimate
- Timeline for completion
- Any special considerations

### Can I walk in without an appointment?
While appointments are preferred, you can inquire about walk-in availability:
- Call ahead: (716) 694-9185
- Flash designs may be available
- Subject to artist availability

### How far in advance should I book?
Booking times vary based on:
- Artist availability
- Project complexity
- Time of year (busier seasons)

We recommend booking as early as possible, especially for custom work or specific dates.

### What if I need to reschedule?
- **48-hour notice required** to reschedule without losing your deposit
- Contact us immediately if you need to change your appointment
- No-shows forfeit the deposit

## Aftercare

### How do I care for my new tattoo?

#### Days 1-5:
1. **Wash gently** with antibacterial soap and lukewarm water
2. **Pat dry** with a clean towel
3. **Apply thin layer** of recommended ointment 2-3Ã— daily
4. **Don't over-moisturize**

#### What to Avoid:
- Direct sunlight
- Swimming pools, hot tubs
- Soaking in bathtubs
- Picking or scratching
- Tight clothing over the tattoo

#### Long-Term:
- Keep moisturized
- Use sunscreen when healed
- Follow studio-provided aftercare instructions

### What ointment should I use?
Your artist will recommend specific aftercare products during your appointment. Common recommendations include:
- Aquaphor
- Hustle Butter
- After Inked
- Tattoo Goo

**Follow your artist's specific instructions.**

### When will my tattoo be fully healed?
- **Surface healing:** 2-3 weeks
- **Complete healing:** 4-6 weeks

Healing time varies based on:
- Tattoo size and location
- Your body's healing rate
- How well you follow aftercare

### What if I have concerns during healing?
Contact the studio immediately:
- **Phone:** (716) 694-9185
- **Email:** admin@americanskinart.com

Signs of concern:
- Excessive redness or swelling
- Unusual discharge
- Fever
- Severe pain

## Preparing for Your Appointment

### How should I prepare?
- **Get rest:** Be well-rested
- **Eat:** Have a meal before arriving
- **Hydrate:** Drink plenty of water
- **Avoid:** Alcohol and blood thinners (unless prescribed)
- **Wear:** Comfortable clothing with easy access to tattoo area
- **Bring:** Valid ID, reference images, positive attitude

### How long will my session take?
Depends on:
- Tattoo size
- Complexity
- Your pain tolerance
- Placement on body

Your artist will provide time estimate during consultation.

### Will it hurt?
Tattoo pain varies by:
- **Placement:** Bony areas tend to be more sensitive
- **Your pain tolerance**
- **Tattoo size and duration**

Most clients describe it as tolerable. Artists work with you to ensure comfort.
